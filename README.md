# JobHarvester 🎯

A job aggregation platform that automatically collects fresh listings from multiple company career pages and presents them through a clean, searchable interface — so job seekers stop wasting time checking sites one by one.

## Live Demo

🌐 [jobharvester-frontend.vercel.app](https://job-harvester-demo.vercel.app/)

---

## What It Does

- Automatically pulls fresh job listings from company career pages on a schedule
- Aggregates everything into one searchable, filterable feed
- Lets users find relevant roles fast — by keyword, experience level, or job type
- Shows full job details with a direct link to apply
